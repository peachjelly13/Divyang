Speed Reading Game

For Students

  • Get your parent or teacher to help you set up the game.
  • Read as much of the text as you can within the time limit.
  • After the timer goes off, your teacher or parent will review and accordingly give you points.
  • Keep Reading as many words you can to get more points.

For Teachers

  • Assist students in setting up the game and understanding the rules.
  • Select the level of the student as can be seen on the top right corner
  • The Child will read out the word and you have to assess if the word has been correctly
  • If the word is spoken correctly click on correct else click skip
  • Encourage students to focus on both speed and comprehension.
  • Provide feedback on their performance to help them improve.
  • Track progress over time to monitor improvement and levels given above.
